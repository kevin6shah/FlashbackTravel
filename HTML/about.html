About Me

Hello, my name is Kevin Shah and I am currently a student at Rutgers University - New Brunswick. During the summer of 2019, I visited Banff & Jasper National Parks in Alberta, Canada on the way from Vancouver to Calgary. Overall, my trip was a great success and I never felt as close to nature as I did there. So I decided to share my story with a bigger audience and highlight some of the best times I had there. As a Computer Science student, I chose to do so by learning some basic web-development and design and building my very first website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I hope the experiences and resources I share with you not only give you an insight on my trip to these beautiful parks, but also assist you in case you plan for such a trip yourself.

Kevin Shah
